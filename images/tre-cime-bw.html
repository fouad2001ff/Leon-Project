Glossy Metal Print (Ready to Hang)

Unlike all my other options which involve mounted paper prints, this popular display option uses a dye sublimation technique to infuse the image into a thin, rigid ChromaLuxe aluminum panel with an ultra glossy finish. The result is a luscious display of vivid colors with excellent clarity, high contrast, and an extraordinary depth of imagery. The aluminum print has an extra 1/8" black dibond backer for added rigidity and durability, and is mounted on a 3/4" deep recessed float frame, so the print appears to float off the wall with no hanging system visible. Acrylic-Glass Fine Art Print (Ready to Hang)

For art collectors who want the best of the best. It starts with the Lumachrome® fine art print with razor sharp details, a subtle metallic sheen, vibrant colors, and lifelike vividness. The print is face-mounted onto a special 1/8" museum-grade, UV protected, scratch-resistant Trulife® acrylic glass with significantly reduced reflectivity; and is backed by a 1/4" black substrate. A robust inset french-cleat hanging system is attached to the back, so that the print appears like a glass framed print is floating on the wall, but without the mat and frame. Bamboo Wood Matte Fine Art Print (Ready to Hang)

Fine Art Print (Loose Print for Framing)

This is the option if you want a fine art print to have matted and framed behind glass. These high quality giclee prints feature vivid, color-accurate ink pigments on heavyweight Canson Infinity Platine Fibre Rag fine art paper with a finely textured, satin semi-gloss surface. The prints have a white border around the image for easy matting and framing. With high archival ratings, these prints will last for many decades without fading.

The loose print is delivered without mounting or framing, so you'll need to handle it with care and take it to a local framer to have it matted and framed as you'd like. (more info)
